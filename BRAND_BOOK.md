# Setlists MD Brand Book (Lydian)

## 1. Core Identity
**Setlists MD** is the intelligent workspace for modern musicians. It is designed for speed, precision, and reliability on stage.

**Lydian** is the name of our Design System. Like the musical mode it's named after, it is bright, uplifting, and structurally sound.

### Brand Voice
- **Professional**: Built for experts who need precision.
- **Minimalist**: Focus on the music, not the interface.
- **Reliable**: Works offline, everywhere, every time.

---

## 2. Design Philosophy: Geist Inspiration
Lydian is heavily inspired by the **Vercel Geist** design system. 
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif and high-utility monospaced fonts.
- **Space**: Generous white space balanced with high-density information grids.
- **Materials**: Flat surfaces with subtle depth provided by 1px borders and soft shadows.

---

## 3. Visual Language

### Primary Color: #53796F
- **Usage**: Brand accents, primary buttons, active states.
- **Tone**: A professional, calm teal that stands out without being distracting.

### Color Scale (1-10)
All components follow a functional mapping from 1 to 10:
- **1-3**: Backgrounds and surfaces.
- **4-6**: Borders and dividers.
- **7-8**: Secondary accents.
- **9-10**: Typography.

### Typography
- **Geist Sans**: The primary font for all headings and body text.
- **Geist Mono**: Used for chords, metadata, and editor components.

---

## 4. Components & Layout
- **Atomic Primitives**: Buttons, Inputs, and Badges found in `src/components/ui`.
- **Materials**:
    - `.material-page`: The base background layer.
    - `.material-card`: Elevated containers with 12px radius.
    - `.material-panel`: Flat containers with 6px radius.
- **Responsive Hierarchy**: Optimized for 100dvh "App-like" experiences on mobile and wide, multi-column layouts on desktop.
