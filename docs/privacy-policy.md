# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [Date]

Welcome to Setlists MD. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Setlists MD application ("the App").

## 1. The Short Version

**We do not collect, store, or sell your data.**

Setlists MD is an "offline-first" application. Your chord charts, setlists, and settings live entirely on your device. We do not have servers that store your musical content, nor do we use analytics software to track your usage.

## 2. Where Your Data Lives

* **Local Storage:** By default, all songs, setlists, and settings you create in the App are stored locally on your device using your browser's IndexedDB storage.
* **Cloud Sync (Optional):** If you choose to enable Cloud Sync, the App connects directly to the cloud storage provider you select (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive).
    * The App will read and write `.md` and `.json` files strictly within the designated `Setlists MD` folder in your cloud drive.
    * This connection happens directly between your device and the cloud provider. Your data **never** passes through or is stored on any server owned or operated by Setlists MD.

## 3. Third-Party Services & OAuth

To provide the optional Cloud Sync feature, Setlists MD uses OAuth to request permission to access your cloud storage.

* **Google Drive / Dropbox / OneDrive:** When you authenticate, you are granting the App permission to manage files *only* within the folder it creates. We do not have access to the rest of your drive, nor do we receive your login credentials (passwords).

## 4. Cookies and Tracking

Setlists MD does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. We do use local browser storage (IndexedDB) solely for the functional purpose of saving your app settings and song data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

If we add new features in the future that require changes to how data is handled (such as an optional, paid, end-to-end encrypted sync service), we will update this policy and notify users within the App.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
