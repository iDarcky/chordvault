# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 29, 2026**

Welcome to **ChordVault** (Setlists MD). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and your rights under global privacy laws, including the GDPR (EU) and CCPA (USA).

## 1. Data We Collect
We only collect data that is strictly necessary for the operation of the app:
*   **Account Information:** Your email address and name (provided via Google/Apple/Email login).
*   **Library Data:** The song titles, lyrics, chords, and setlists you create or import.
*   **Team Information:** If you join or create a team, we store your role and membership status.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
Your data is used exclusively to:
*   Provide the Setlists MD service (syncing your library across devices).
*   Facilitate team collaboration.
*   Verify your subscription status.

**We NEVER sell your data to third parties.**

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We use **Supabase** (a professional cloud database provider) to store your information. 
*   **Location:** Data is stored in secure data centers (typically in the EU or US depending on your region).
*   **Security:** All data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)
Regardless of where you live, we provide the following rights to all users:
*   **Access & Portability:** You can export your song library at any time via the "Export" feature.
*   **Right to Rectification:** You can edit your profile and songs directly in the app.
*   **Right to Erasure (The "Right to be Forgotten"):** You may delete your account at any time. This will permanently wipe your personal library and account information from our active databases.

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)
ChordVault is intended for use by worship leaders and musicians. You must be **13 years of age or older** to create an account. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 6. Copyright Responsibility
ChordVault is a storage and organization tool. 
*   Users are responsible for ensuring they have the legal right or license (e.g., CCLI, SongSelect) to store and display copyrighted material (lyrics and chords) within the app.
*   We act as a passive storage provider and do not monitor your private library for content.

## 7. Cookie Policy
We use "strictly necessary" local storage and session tokens to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
