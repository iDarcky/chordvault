# Setlists MD: The Product Pitch

## The Problem
For worship leaders, music directors, and musicians, managing chord charts is often a frustrating experience.
- You use PDFs, which are static. If a vocalist needs the song in a different key 10 minutes before service, you have to find a computer, transpose the chart, generate a new PDF, and distribute it.
- You use apps like OnSong, which are expensive, platform-locked (iOS only), and complex.
- You use Planning Center, which is fantastic for scheduling, but locks your charts behind an internet connection, a subscription, and their specific ecosystem.

## The Solution: Setlists MD
**Setlists MD is the portable, offline-first chord chart app that puts you back in control of your music.**

Think of it as the "Obsidian" for worship teams. Instead of proprietary databases or static PDFs, your songs are simple, future-proof `.md` (Markdown) files. Setlists MD takes those files and renders them into beautiful, dynamic chord charts.

### Why Musicians and Leaders Love It:

#### 1. True Data Ownership
Your songs aren't locked in a walled garden. They live as plain text files on your device or in your own Google Drive/Dropbox. You can edit them in any text editor. If Setlists MD disappeared tomorrow, you would still have your entire library, perfectly readable.

#### 2. Dynamic & Flexible
- **Instant Transposition:** Change the key of any song with a tap.
- **Capo Calculator:** Playing in Bb but want to use G shapes? The app calculates it instantly.
- **Nashville Numbers:** Toggle between standard chords and Nashville Number System on the fly.
- **Modulations:** Built-in support for mid-song key changes that flow seamlessly.

#### 3. Zero Friction
- **No Accounts Required:** You don't need to sign up to use the core app. Just open it and go.
- **Works Offline:** Internet down at church? No problem. Setlists MD is an offline-first app. Your entire library lives on your device.
- **Cross-Platform:** Because it's a Progressive Web App (PWA), it runs perfectly on iPads, Android tablets, phones, and desktops.

#### 4. Team Collaboration Simplified
No need to manage user seats or complex permissions. Connect Setlists MD to a shared Google Drive folder. When the Music Director updates a chart or builds a setlist, it syncs directly to the band's devices.

### The Bottom Line
Setlists MD removes the friction of managing music. It’s free, lightning-fast, and respects your data. It’s the tool built for the musician on stage who just needs their charts to *work*.
